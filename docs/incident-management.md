
# Incident Management Process

## Severity Levels

### P1
Complete outage affecting customers.

### P2
Major degradation.

### P3
Minor degradation.

### P4
Informational.

## Incident Lifecycle

1. Detection
2. Triage
3. Mitigation
4. Resolution
5. Postmortem

## Mandatory Requirements

- Incident commander
- Timeline tracking
- Communication updates
- Blameless postmortem
